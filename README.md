## About Me

I am **Mohammad Barabadi**, a Master's student in **Autonomy Technologies** at **Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU)**, with a background in **Electrical Engineering** from **Amirkabir University of Technology**.

My work and interests are focused on the intersection of **robotics**, **embedded systems**, **control systems**, **signal processing**, and **intelligent autonomous systems**. I enjoy developing engineering projects that connect software, hardware, sensors, control logic, and real-world implementation.
